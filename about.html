About

  • Mouse movements handled.

  • Hands free interaction.
  • Ease of use.

INSTRUCTIONS TO USE

Scroll

Keep the right eye closed and move the head in direction of scrolling.

Drag

Keep the left eye closed and move the head in proper direction.

Double click

Blink left eye twice within two seconds.

Mouse Reposition

Keep both the eyes closed for two seconds.

Left click

Blink left eye once.

Right Click

Blink right eye once.
